Introduction

In this section, you’ll learn about the importance of having a portfolio website, who should have one, and why. We’ll explore examples of successful portfolios and understand how they create impact.

Key takeaway: A great portfolio is your digital first impression.

Project Setup

Set up your environment with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Organize your files into folders for easy management. Create a main file called `index.html` for your website’s homepage.

Tip: Use a version control system like Git for backups and collaboration.

Design Choices

Choose a design style that reflects your personal brand. Decide on a color palette, typography, and layout that resonates with your goals.

Pro Tip: Use tools like Figma or Canva to prototype your design before coding.
